# Privacy Policy — Discount Capper

Effective date: March 31, 2026  
Last updated: March 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how **Lincoln Murray (individual app provider)** ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and discloses information when merchants install and use the **Discount Capper** Shopify app (the "App").

## 1. Scope

This policy applies to information processed through the App in connection with Shopify stores that install the App.

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following information to operate the App:

- Shopify store information (for example, shop domain and app installation/session data).
- Shopify app session information required for authentication and app operation (which can include store/admin account metadata provided by Shopify, such as name, email, locale, and access scopes).
- App configuration data set by the merchant (for example, discount percentage and maximum discount cap values).
- Technical and diagnostic information (for example, logs needed to monitor reliability, security, and troubleshooting).

### Customer personal data

Discount Capper is designed to configure and apply discount rules.  
We do **not** intentionally collect or store customer personal data unless required for lawful support/security purposes or provided by Shopify platform workflows.

## 3. How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Authenticate and maintain app sessions.
- Provide core app functionality (discount cap configuration and execution).
- Maintain app security, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot issues.
- Comply with legal obligations and Shopify platform requirements.

## 4. Legal Basis (where applicable)

Where required by law, we process personal data based on one or more of the following:

- Performance of a contract (providing the App to merchants).
- Legitimate interests (security, fraud prevention, product improvement).
- Compliance with legal obligations.
- Consent, where required.

## 5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell merchant or customer personal information.  
We may share data with:

- Service providers that help us host, monitor, and operate the App (under confidentiality and data protection obligations).
- Shopify, as required for app functionality and platform operations.
- Legal or regulatory authorities when required by law.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy, including legal, accounting, and security needs.

When an app is uninstalled, we delete or anonymize applicable app data within 30 days, unless longer retention is required by law.

## 7. Data Security

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
